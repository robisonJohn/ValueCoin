About Velocity

The purpose of this application is to encourage intelligent investing in the space of crypto assets by synthesizing a tremendous quantity of market data into comprehensible quantitiatve metrics and visualizations. Presently, it is in its beta form. As the application grows, the goal is to create an intuitive user-interface which enables users to make buy and sell orders in real time based on the user's knowledge, interest, time investment, risk-tolerance, etc. As accessibility grows within the crypto space, so it will shift away from highly volatile "punting" and towards reasoned market decisions that create more stable and trustworthy markets. This, in turn, will facilitate mainstream adoption for this revolutionary technology.
