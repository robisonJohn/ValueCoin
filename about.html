About the Developer

This application has been developed by John Robison, a senior at Columbia University studying mathematics and statistics. His primary interests include number theory and its applications to mathematical cryptography, behavorial economics, blockchain technology, and machine learning. The goal of this application is two-fold: to serve as a means to make cryptoasset market data more accessible to the average investor; and also to encourage the notion of value investing in the crypto space. He has held roles at NASA conducting research into statistical machine learning methods for climate research (2020) and at the Blockchain Research Institute exploring DeFi and its various implications in greater depth (2021-Present). In his free time, he enjoys science fiction and fantasy in all its various mediums (books, films, video games, etc.), running in Central Park, playing with dogs, and exploring the mobius strip that is New York City.
